The Challenge

A Crisis Hidden in Plain Sight

Millions of neurodivergent children in India lack access to early screening and personalized support, leading to delayed interventions and missed developmental milestones.

1-3%

Autism Spectrum Disorder

Children in India affected by ASD struggle with communication and emotional regulation without personalized support.

5-15%

ADHD Symptoms

Symptomatic children face poor task completion, careless mistakes, and difficulty following multi-step instructions.

2.1-3‰

Cerebral Palsy

Per 1,000 live births in India — children need continuous motor skills monitoring and adaptive therapy.

6-12%

Dyslexia (SLD)

Children with specific learning disabilities face mirror confusion, direction lag, and phonological processing issues.

⚠️

The Harsh Reality

General education teachers have little to no training in autism intervention. Most neurodivergent students are placed in general classrooms without proper support systems.
